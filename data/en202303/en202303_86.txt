5 Conclusions
In this paper , we investigate the beamforming design un ‐
der channel prediction errors in a time -varying MIMO sys ‐
tem. By collecting the prediction errors and exploiting the 
statistical characteristics of the error samples , we propose a 
robust beamforming design to further combat the detriment 
caused by channel aging based on the channel prediction sys ‐
tem. Due to the uncertainty brought by the error part , we ex ‐
ploit deterministic equivalents to obtain the closed -form ex ‐
pression to derive the robust beamforming matrix . Our simu ‐
lation results show the effectiveness of the proposed beam ‐
forming design and reveal that the achievable gain even 
grows as UE velocities increase . The proposed design can maintain the outperformance during the DL transmission 
time while the channels vary fast .
